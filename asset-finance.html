Asset Finance

Overview

Sedfa offers asset finance solutions tailored to your business’ needs. We help you to finance a wide range of new and/or used moveable assets.

The money provided for asset finance is used to acquire business moveable assets (machinery and equipment). Assets purchased through this funding method must be identifiable by a means of serial numbers.

Benefits of asset loans

  • Asset finance allows you to acquire or lease equipment without affecting your bank balance.
  • Assets acquired through asset finance can be used by Sedfa as security for the loan

Repayment term

The loan can be taken for up to a maximum of 60 months or for the economic life of the asset – whichever comes first.
